Meet Teacher Pat

Hello! I'm Patricia Naicker, also known as Teacher Pat — an Online English Teacher, Communication Specialist, and Confidence Coach based in Johannesburg, South Africa.

I’m passionate about helping learners of all ages become confident, eloquent English speakers. I bring over a decade of experience in corporate communications and am TEFL-certified with hands-on experience tutoring children and adults.

Why Learn With Me?

With my multicultural background and fluency in multiple languages, I create a nurturing, learner-centered environment tailored to your unique goals. My lessons focus on practical communication skills, confidence-building, and cultural understanding, so you can speak English eloquently and authentically anywhere in the world.
